# Selene Chat Icon Design

**Date**: 2025-11-15
**Purpose**: App icon design specification for AI generation
**Style**: Liquid glass UI with night sky theme

---

## Design Overview

A three-dimensional crescent moon rendered in luminous liquid glass, floating against a rich night sky with silver stars. The icon combines warm metallic tones (silver and gold) with cool navy blues to create a sophisticated, calming aesthetic that represents Selene Chat's privacy-focused, ADHD-optimized knowledge management purpose.

---

## Core Visual Elements

### The Crescent Moon

**Material**: Liquid glass with metallic properties
- Primary color: Lustrous silver
- Accent color: Subtle warm gold highlights along edges and inner curve
- Finish: Translucent with glossy surface

**3D Characteristics**:
- **Translucent depth**: Multiple subtle layers visible within the glass with light refracting through
- **Surface reflections**: Glossy highlights suggesting smooth, flowing surface
- **Soft inner glow**: Luminescence emanating from within the glass material
- **Edge definition**: Crisp but slightly rounded edges where glass material terminates

**Orientation**:
- Positioned at 3/4 perspective angle (not flat-on)
- Shows dimensionality as if viewing a sculpture floating in space
- Horns pointing toward upper-right
- Creates ascending, forward-moving energy

### The Night Sky Background

**Base Colors**:
- Deep, rich navy blue gradient
- Darker midnight blue at edges
- Slightly lighter royal blue behind the moon
- Creates depth and focus on the moon

**Stars**:
- 15-25 stars of varying sizes and brightness
- Delicate silver color with subtle shimmer quality
- Largest stars: Four-pointed sparkle effects
- Medium stars: Simple bright dots with slight silver-to-white gradient
- Smallest stars: Soft dots, some slightly out of focus for depth
- Distribution: Scattered throughout, at varying distances

---

## Lighting & Atmosphere

### Light Sources

**Primary Light**: The crescent moon itself
- Creates beautiful contrast with dark navy sky
- Acts as the composition's luminous focal point

**Rim Lighting**:
- Bright silver-gold light traces along outer edge of crescent
- Makes moon pop against navy background
- Most intense along the curve

**Internal Glow**:
- Strongest at thickest part of crescent (the curve)
- Light appears to pool within the liquid glass
- Radiates outward with decreasing intensity

**Ambient Illumination**:
- Moon casts subtle silver glow onto surrounding sky
- Soft halo or atmospheric effect
- Fades gradually into navy blue

### Transparency & Depth

**Glass Transparency**:
- Thinnest areas (near horn tips): More night sky visible through glass
- Starlight filters through translucent sections
- Creates layered depth effect

**Atmospheric Effects**:
- Subtle shimmer particles or bokeh effects floating near moon
- Like magical dust catching moonlight
- Stars at varying depths (some crisp, others softly blurred)
- Color temperature gradient: warm silver-gold moon vs. cool navy blue sky

---

## Material Details

### Liquid Glass Finish

**Core Structure**:
- Frosted semi-translucent quality in main body
- Crystal-clear at edges
- Creates contrast and definition

**Metallic Sheen**:
- Silver base throughout
- Warm gold accents concentrated where light hits most intensely
- Smooth transitions between colors

**Refraction Effects**:
- Subtle color splitting (prism-like) where glass is thickest
- Adds realism and visual interest
- Reinforces liquid glass material

**Organic Qualities**:
- Tiny bubbles within the glass
- Subtle swirls in the material
- Hand-crafted feel, not sterile or perfect
- Adds warmth and personality

## Technical Specifications

### Composition (1024x1024px macOS standard)

**Moon Placement**:
- Slightly off-center positioning
- Occupies 60-70% of icon space
- Visual weight anchored in lower-left to center area
- Horn tips reach toward upper-right

**Framing**:
- Navy sky extends to all edges
- Breathing room around moon
- Moon floats with space to glow
- All critical elements within macOS safe zone (accounts for rounded corner mask)

### Color Palette

**Moon**:
- Base: Lustrous silver (#C0C0C0 to #E8E8E8 range)
- Highlights: Warm gold (#FFD700 to #FFA500 range)
- Glow: Soft white with slight warm tint

**Sky**:
- Dark edge: Midnight blue (#191970, #0F1B4C)
- Center: Royal blue (#1E3A8A, #2C5282)
- Smooth gradient transition

**Stars**:
- Silver (#C0C0C0 to #FFFFFF)
- Brightest stars: Pure white centers
- Subtle shimmer: Very light blue tint (#F0F8FF)

## Design Rationale

**Privacy & Focus**: The self-illuminating moon represents Selene Chat's local-first, privacy-focused nature - it generates its own light rather than depending on external sources.

**ADHD Optimization**: The liquid glass aesthetic with organic imperfections creates visual interest without overwhelming. The floating moon in calm night sky suggests clarity and focus.

**Sophistication**: Silver and gold metallics convey premium quality and trustworthiness appropriate for a personal knowledge management tool.

**Selene Connection**: Named after the Greek goddess of the moon, the crescent moon is the perfect symbolic representation while being immediately recognizable and memorable.

**macOS Native Feel**: Liquid glass and depth effects align with modern macOS design language while standing out with unique metallic warmth.
